OsloMets website for international students does not represents international environment in an exiting way. Social activites shown on the website are few and not inviting.



To test the hypotese a survey was conducted from the Facebook group "International Students at OsloMet".
The result of the survey is presented below:







Question 1. I think that layout promotes social activities in a way that makes me curious to know more?





Question 2. I think the website shows a varied range of activities?





Question 3. How many times have you visited OsloMets International Student Website this past months?





The results of the survey show that 92.9% of the international students use the OsloMet website for international students.
However, the students are not satisfied with how the activities are presented on the website.
The range of activities must be multiple and presented in an exciting way.
